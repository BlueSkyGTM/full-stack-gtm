A three-agent AI pipeline that reads job listings and funding signals, classifies the operational pressure behind them, and produces Schwartz-style outreach grounded in each prospect's specific situation. Built on Node.js, stress-tested to 1,500 cycles, broken 18 times, and rebuilt on Google ADK with Vertex AI RAG.

V1 and V2 are fully interactive — nodes are clickable, rows are selectable, agent prompts are readable. V3 unlocks when V2 Phase 2 ships.
