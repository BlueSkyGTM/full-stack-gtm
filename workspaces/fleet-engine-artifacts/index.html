The Crew
🐋 Ahab
Find · Discovery
Hunts job listings and funding signals via Vertex AI Grounding. Runs 5+ search queries per campaign. Applies company-level filters, excludes aggregator URLs, and builds the Catch array. Cross-campaign deduplication happens automatically via RAG.
🔬 Nemo
Enrich · Intelligence
Takes each raw lead from Ahab and dives deep. Verifies the company URL, identifies a decision-maker, classifies the friction type (API Stutter / Scale Friction / Manual Data Debt / Displacement Signal), and applies CATALYST_STALE — any funding signal older than 18 months routes to shipwrecked, never reaches Neptune.
🔱 Neptune
Synthesize · Outreach
Receives Nemo's friction profile. Writes a 3–4 sentence Schwartz-style Outreach Bite — reflects what the prospect is already doing, names the specific villain (the tool or process failing them), and offers the exact operational outcome they already want. responseSchema enforcement means Neptune cannot return prose.
